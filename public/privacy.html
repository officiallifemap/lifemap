Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

The short version: we collect only what's needed to run the app. We never sell your data, never connect to your bank, and never show you ads. Your data is yours.

What we collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used to identify your account and send password reset emails if requested.
  • Display name and profile photo — if provided via Google or Yahoo sign-in, or set by you directly.

When you use lifemap, you may add personal information including goals, habits, financial figures, life events, to-do items, notes, wellness data, and details about people in your life. This is all user-generated content — we store it only to power the app for you.

What we don't collect

  • We do not connect to your bank or any financial institution.
  • We do not use advertising trackers or analytics.
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal data in any way.
  • We do not use AI to process or analyze your content.
  • Profile photos and event images you upload are stored locally on your device only and are never sent to our servers.

How your data is stored

lifemap uses Firebase (by Google) for authentication and cloud storage. Your account data and app content are stored in Firebase Firestore, hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure in the United States.

Firebase's privacy practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

If you use the app without creating an account, your data is stored only in your browser's local storage on your device and is never sent to any server.

How we use your data

  • To sync your lifemap data across your devices when you're signed in.
  • To send password reset emails when you request them.
  • That's it.

Your rights

You can:

  • Delete your data — use the "Clear all data" option in your profile settings, or contact us and we'll remove it from our servers within 30 days.
  • Delete your account — contact us at officiallifemap@gmail.com and we'll delete your account and all associated data.
  • Stop syncing — sign out and continue using the app locally at any time. Your local data remains on your device.

Children

lifemap is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we make meaningful changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page. We won't reduce your privacy rights without giving you a way to know.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us: officiallifemap@gmail.com
